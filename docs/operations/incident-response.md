# Incident Response

## Severity Model

- Sev-1: major outage or data integrity risk.
- Sev-2: significant degradation with workaround.
- Sev-3: minor impact and no immediate revenue risk.

## Response Flow

1. Detect and triage.
2. Assign incident commander.
3. Stabilize service.
4. Communicate status updates.
5. Resolve and verify.
6. Publish post-incident review.

## Owner-Facing Communication

For Sev-1 and Sev-2 incidents, provide updates with:

- impact summary,
- expected recovery timeline,
- mitigation actions,
- and preventive follow-up plan.
