# Presentation Narrative

Use this structure for a 10-15 minute founder, investor, or stakeholder presentation.

## 1. Opening: The Pain

"Growth teams are not short on tools. They are short on coordination and accountable execution."

## 2. Problem

- SEO, ads, content, and reviews are managed in silos.
- Decision cycles are slow.
- Budgets leak before teams respond.

## 3. Solution

GrowthSO is a unified AI operating system that converts fragmented growth work into a measurable execution loop.

## 4. Product Demo Story

- Onboard domain and channels.
- Run baseline diagnosis.
- Receive ranked action queue.
- Execute with human approval.
- Track KPI impact in one dashboard.

## 5. Differentiation

- Cross-channel action orchestration, not single-tool analytics.
- Owner-first KPI governance.
- Automation that remains auditable and controllable.

## 6. Business Model

- Starter: $29
- Pro: $99
- Agency: $299
- Enterprise expansion via SLA and governance add-ons.

## 7. Execution Plan

- 90-day MVP with measurable proof points.
- Scale path to multi-tenant 1M-user architecture.

## 8. Closing

GrowthSO is not another marketing dashboard. It is the operating layer for predictable growth.
