# Pitch Deck Outline

This page preserves and expands the legacy pitch deck structure into a production-owner narrative.

## Slide Flow

1. Vision
2. Problem
3. Solution
4. Product
5. Market
6. Technology
7. Business model
8. Growth strategy
9. Competition
10. Future vision

## Key Talking Points

- Problem: growth teams are fragmented across disconnected tools.
- Solution: a unified operating system for SEO, content, reputation, and ads.
- Technology: scalable microservices and AI decision loops.
- Business model: recurring SaaS with expansion paths.
- Growth strategy: land with MVP outcomes, expand through automation.
