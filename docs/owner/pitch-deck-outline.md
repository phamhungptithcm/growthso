# Pitch Deck Outline

Use this as the canonical deck flow for fundraising, partner discussions, or internal strategy alignment.

## Slide 1: Vision

GrowthSO is the AI Growth Operating System for businesses that want predictable digital growth with less operational complexity.

## Slide 2: Problem

Growth execution is fragmented across SEO, ads, content, and review systems, causing slow decisions and wasted spend.

## Slide 3: Solution

One platform to detect opportunities, prioritize actions, execute workflows, and measure results.

## Slide 4: Product

Web + mobile platform with modules for:

- SEO intelligence
- AI content
- ads budget optimization
- review and reputation automation
- owner KPI command center

## Slide 5: Market

Large and expanding demand from SMBs, agencies, and multi-location operators needing ROI-focused growth operations.

## Slide 6: Technology

AI-assisted orchestration on scalable microservices, event pipelines, and production-ready cloud infrastructure.

## Slide 7: Business Model

Tiered SaaS pricing with expansion through automation depth and enterprise governance add-ons.

## Slide 8: GTM Strategy

Land via high-friction growth pain points, expand via cross-channel workflow adoption.

## Slide 9: Competition

Differentiate by execution loop and owner accountability, not feature parity with single-channel tools.

## Slide 10: Roadmap and Ask

Present 90-day MVP goals, next 12-month scale milestones, and required resources to accelerate growth.
