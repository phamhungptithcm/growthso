# Success Metrics & Governance

## North Star

Qualified pipeline and revenue growth driven by measurable, repeatable growth operations.

## KPI Framework

### Acquisition KPIs

- Organic sessions growth.
- Share of voice for priority keywords.
- Cost per acquisition (paid + blended).

### Activation KPIs

- Lead-to-opportunity conversion rate.
- Time from insight to campaign change.
- Content production cycle time.

### Retention & Efficiency KPIs

- MRR retention by customer segment.
- Review response SLA adherence.
- Marketing efficiency ratio.

## Governance Cadence

- Weekly: operational performance and blockers.
- Monthly: budget efficiency and roadmap adjustments.
- Quarterly: strategy reset and investment decisions.

## Ownership Model

- Production owner: outcome accountability.
- Product lead: capability prioritization.
- Engineering lead: reliability and delivery quality.
- Growth operator: execution and experimentation.
